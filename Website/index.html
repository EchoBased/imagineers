Imagineers of tommorow

Here at Disney, we understand that there is a creator in all of us. We all have unique and interesting stories to tell. Through our program we want to light the spark of creativity in the next generation of storytellers. Through several modules we are sure young creators will find their creative niche and hopefully with a little magic and a guiding hand from our Imagineers your children will become aspiring creators. When Imagineers complete the course, they will have a fundamental understanding of a variety of creative tools to chase a creative career.
