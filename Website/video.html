Movie Makers

At Disney videography is huge and has been used for decades and will continue to be used in our production for years. It is the basis of filmmaking and storytelling. Videography can be found in countless Disney Movies and shows that involve human characters or a real life setting. In areas where animation may not want to be used or can't be used, videography is used to capture and create Disney films and shows from Star Wars to Marvel. Understanding what it takes to produce a great piece of video media will be a great foundation helping your kid grow their artistic and creative abilities along with having the basic tools to create successful imagery using a camera will only boost their skills towards becoming successful artists of film production.

Tools of the trade

Your kids will learn the basics of videography with our program by learning the basics of how to use and handle a camera as a creative tool. Learn how to capture certain imagery, feelings, or a certain setting on film and capture the magic. They will understand the basics of lighting and sound which is crucial to successful videography. By the end of this module of the program they will be able to successfully produce a well composed and creative piece of video, which can be used in story telling.
